About Glassmorphism Generator

Glassmorphism CSS Generator is a free online tool built for designers and developers who want to create beautiful, modern glass-like UI elements without writing complex CSS from scratch. Our mission is to make glassmorphism accessible to everyone — from beginners learning CSS to experienced developers prototyping dashboards.

What We Offer

Live Preview: See your glassmorphism effect update in real-time as you adjust sliders. No refresh needed — just drag and watch.

Multiple Formats: Export your styles as plain CSS, Tailwind classes, or SCSS variables. Whatever your workflow, we've got you covered.

Preset Gallery: Browse 10+ ready-made styles from Frost to Neon Glass. Click any preset to load it instantly in the generator.

Shareable URLs: Every configuration generates a unique URL. Share your exact glass style with teammates or save it for later.

Our Story

Glassmorphism Generator started as a personal project to solve a simple problem: creating consistent glass effects across different projects was tedious. What began as a weekend experiment grew into a tool used by designers and developers worldwide.

We believe great design tools should be free, fast, and frustration-free. No sign-ups, no paywalls, no bloat — just a clean interface that gets the job done.

Why Glassmorphism?

Glassmorphism has become a staple of modern UI design — used by Apple, Microsoft, Spotify, and countless other brands. It creates depth, feels premium, and works beautifully on dark backgrounds. Our tool helps you harness this trend without the trial-and-error of manual CSS tweaking.

Whether you're building a dashboard, a landing page, or a mobile app, glassmorphism can elevate your design. And we're here to make that process as smooth as possible.
